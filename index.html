About

I'm Nicholas, a senior at UMass Amherst studying Psychology with a minor in Business. I spent this past spring studying at the Institut Catholique de Paris, which is where most of these photos were shot.

At UMass AdLab I work as Project Manager, leading teams of 15 to 20 students on campaigns for real clients. Last summer I interned at Matter Surfaces in marketing, working on copywriting, brand consistency, and web content.
